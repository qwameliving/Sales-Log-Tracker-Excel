# Sales-Log-Tracker-Excel
An Excel-based system to track product sales across multiple products, with automated summaries and entry validation.
SalesLog-Tracker is an Excel-based system designed to manage and monitor daily product sales efficiently across multiple product lines. It helps sales teams and managers track sales performance, prevent incorrect entries, and provide instant summaries of total sales.

Key Features:**
- Tracks daily sales for multiple products.
- Validates entries to prevent errors.
- Automatically summarizes sales in a summary sheet.
- Provides a clear view of total sales and sales per product.
- Easy to use interface suitable for teams of any size.


Audience / Users: 
1. Sales teams
2. Branch managers
3. Business analysts who want a reliable tool to monitor daily sales.
